Frequently Asked Questions

Is this safe for first-time Mexico visitors?

Yes. We specialize in supporting international guests with local guidance and optional transportation. Our area is safe and we provide all necessary support to ensure a comfortable, worry-free stay.

Do I need a rental car?

No. Airport pickup and local transportation are available as optional services. We can arrange everything from airport transfers to daily transportation for your chosen experiences.

How do I book?

Simply message us on WhatsApp at 307-431-8328 or email bighorggrp@gmail.com to check availability. We'll discuss your needs, confirm dates, and send you a booking contract.

What's included in the guided experiences?

Our private guide will pick you up, provide transportation, offer cultural and historical context at each location, handle tickets and reservations, and ensure you have a memorable, stress-free experience. The $300/day is for the entire group (up to 12 people), not per person.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer a flexible cancellation policy: full refund if cancelled 30+ days before arrival, 50% refund if cancelled 14-29 days before, and no refund if cancelled less than 14 days before. We recommend travel insurance for unexpected circumstances.

Ready to plan your stay near the Popo?

Contact us directly to check availability and create your perfect itinerary. We'll respond within 24 hours.
